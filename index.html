How to Watch Movies

  1. Browse the categories or use the search bar.
  2. Click on a movie card to open its details.
  3. Click 'Watch Now' on the movie details screen.
  4. You may need to watch a short ad before the movie starts. Click 'Watch Ad to Play'.
  5. After watching the ad, click 'Play Movie' to start streaming!
